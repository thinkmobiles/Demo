HOW DO YOU PERSONALIZE A DEMO?

START WITH THE BASICS

Work with us to develop an affordable video that describes your product or service. Include the points you feel will be important to your prospects.

GATHER FEEDBACK

The best salespeople are great listeners. DemoRocket gets interactive with your prospects asking them what services or features they’re interested in hearing more about.

DIG IN

Armed with an understanding of what’s important, DemoRocket delivers more detailed information in the form of specific videos and downloadable PDFs.

ANALYZE

DemoRocket’s analytics allow you to see what’s working in real time to help you reach the prospect while you’re top of mind and easily make changes to your message based on what you’ve learned about them.
