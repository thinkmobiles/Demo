PRICING

DemoRocket is the market’s most affordable way to showcase your product or service, learn what your prospects want and close more deals. DemoRocket’s pricing is super flexible and designed to allow anyone to try it.

The “Quick Hit” Package

The quick hit is a 3 month campaign that provides you with:

  • A DemoRocket account for up to 3 users
  • Full access to the automated sales demo analytics reports
  • Daily summary reports
  • Immediate email notifications when interested prospects are identified

Pricing for the quick hit starts at $1,500 per month*

The “Getting Serious” Package

Getting Serious is a 6 month campaign that provides you with:

  • A DemoRocket account for up to 5 users
  • Full access to the automated sales demo analytics reports
  • Daily summary reports
  • Immediate email notifications when interested prospects are identified

Pricing for getting serious starts at $1,000 per month.*

The Long Haul Package

The long haul is a 12 month commitment that provides you with:

  • A DemoRocket account with up to 10 users
  • The ability to run up to 5 campaigns simultaneously
  • Full access to the automated sales demo analytics reports
  • Daily summary reports
  • Immediate email notifications when interested prospects are identified

Pricing for the long haul starts at $900 per month*
